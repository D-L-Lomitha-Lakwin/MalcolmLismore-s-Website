~Malcolm's Photo Gallery~


Welcome to the Photo Gallery of Malcolm Lismore Photography.
This gallery showcases a stunning collection of images that capture the essence of the rugged Scottish landscape,
its natural wildlife, and coastal beauty. From breathtaking sunsets over mountainous terrains to candid moments of native wildlife,
each photo tells a story of Scotland’s untamed charm. Explore our curated selection of landscape and wildlife photography,
as well as personal event shoots, including weddings and portraits. Whether you're looking for inspiration, or to relive a memory, this gallery is a visual journey through Malcolm’s artistic lens. Enjoy the moments we've captured for you!
