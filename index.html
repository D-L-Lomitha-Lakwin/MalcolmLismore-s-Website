With a deep passion for the natural world, my work primarily focuses on capturing the stunning Scottish landscapes, native wildlife, and coastal birds. While my love for nature photography is evident, my versatility allows me to also offer my skills for weddings, portraits, and other special events, making me a sought-after photographer for a range of occasions. Through my photography, I aim to showcase both the beauty of nature and the personal moments that matter to my clients.
